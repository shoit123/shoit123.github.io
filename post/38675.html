1.6 注意事项

  1. 所有服务启动过程中报错

​ hadoop01: Permission denied (publickey,gssapi-keyex,gssapi-with-mic,password).

​ 表示ssh没有连接成功，自己尝试ssh连接有问题的节点，包括localhost也需要ssh-copy-id

  1. 启动过程中报错

    hadoop02: secondarynamenode is running as process 18113. Stop it first and ensure /tmp/hadoop-root-secondarynamenode.pid file is empty before retry.

    表示已经有进程占用，则需要先停止服务并重新启动
